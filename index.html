How to Install

Step 1: Pay ₹300 using UPI (PhonePe or GPay) via the form below.
Step 2: After successful payment, click “Request APK” to chat with our Telegram bot.
Step 3: The bot triggers a GitHub workflow that builds a custom KidsLogger APK for you.
Step 4: Install the APK, enable Accessibility permission, and you're good to go!
